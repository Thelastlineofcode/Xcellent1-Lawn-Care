# Owner Vision — LaCardio Interview

---

## The End Game

LaCardio's vision is a **self-regulating business**. The app replaces direct manager-to-worker communication for accountability, performance, and procedure. He shouldn't have to tell crew what they already know — the app tells them.

> *"I ain't got to tell you your performance because the app gonna tell you. The app got you in red."*

---

## How It Should Work

### 1. Owner — Real-Time Business Eyes

**Desired Behavior:**
The owner is away doing other things. The moment a job is completed, he gets a notification. He can decide whether to go check the job that day or not. He doesn't need to open a dashboard to find out what's happening.

**End State:**
- Push notification fires the instant a job is marked complete
- Notification includes: client, address, crew, job type
- Owner can tap to view photos directly from the notification
- No polling, no manual refresh

---

### 2. Crew — Accountability Without a Conversation

**Desired Behavior:**
A crew member opens the app and immediately knows where they stand. If they've been slacking — late to jobs, missing photos, low completion rate — the app is already telling them they're in the red. The owner doesn't have to say anything.

**End State:**
- Crew dashboard shows a performance status: 🟢 On Track / 🟡 Watch Out / 🔴 You're in the Red
- Status is based on: job completion rate, on-time arrival, photos uploaded
- The color is visible before the crew member does anything else
- Owner sets the thresholds; crew cannot edit their own status

---

### 3. Crew — Follow the Procedure, Not a Phone Call

**Desired Behavior:**
When a crew member is assigned a job, they see exactly what steps to follow — in order. The app creates the procedure so the owner doesn't have to explain it every time. Photos are required at specific steps.

**End State:**
- Every job has an SOP checklist the crew works through step by step
- Owner builds SOP templates per job type (e.g., "Weekly Maintenance", "Bush Hog")
- Crew cannot mark a job complete until required steps and photos are done
- Owner sees checklist completion alongside job status in the dashboard
