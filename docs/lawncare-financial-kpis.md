# Industry Benchmarks and Financial KPIs – Xcellent 1 Lawn Care Context (2025)

## Market Benchmarks (Landscaping & Lawn Care)

- **Annual Revenue (per company, median):**
  - Solo/very small (1–2 crew, owner-operator): $65K–$220K
  - Small team scale (3–6 FTE): $250K–$600K
  - Regional/established (6+ FTE, 2+ crews): $700K–$2.5M
- **Year-over-year growth (2025):** 3%–6% projected (national)
- **Industry Profit Margin:**
  - Net profit margin: 10%–14% typical (FieldCamp 2025)
  - Top performers 15–20%
  - Solo/owner-operator: sometimes lower due to self-employment tax
- **Gross margin:** 35–45% for residential mowing packages, 40–55% for
  commercial/HOA contracts, 50–60% for add-ons (aeration, landscaping)

## Financial KPI Benchmarks

- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):**
  - 60–80% of revenue in best-run companies comes from recurring contracts
- **Annual customer retention rate:** 75–80% is industry avg; >90% for top
  operators
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
  - $25–60/residential, $150–600/commercial; ROI if recouped in 3–6 months
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** $1,800–$4,800 typical residential;
  $8,000+ for commercial ($200×12–24 months recurring)
- **Revenue per crew per week:** $2,000–$4,200 depending on service mix, routing
  efficiency
- **Revenue per FTE per year:** $70K–$110K average
- **On-time job completion rate:** >90% best practice
- **Churn/cancellation rate:** <5% monthly (higher if not on subscription model)
- **Review acquisition rate:** 8–15/month for top quartile local businesses

## Operational Metrics

- **Average jobs per crew per day:** 6–14 (depends on size, density)
- **Average drive time/route density:** <20 min total between stops per day is
  ideal for highest margin
- **Labor as % of revenue:** 35–55% (biggest variable cost)
- **Equipment repairs/replacement as % of revenue:** 4–8%

## Laplace/Regional Cost/Revenue Notes

- **Wages (Laplace)**: $15–$22/hr for crew; supervisors $22–$28/hr; owner draw =
  net profit
- **Market Pricing:** See business model file (previous report)
- **Seasonality:** Revenue can dip 30–35% in Dec–Feb; stabilize with recurring
  contracts, seasonal add-ons
- **Compliance:** Liability, workers comp, licensing required – see Compliance
  Report

## Xcellent 1 Targets (recommended for scaling phase)

- **Net profit (after all expenses):** Aim 12–15%
- **Recurring revenue %:** >70% (protect cash flow, reduce marketing strain)
- **Retention target:** >85%/year
- **Review growth:** +10/mo
- **Crew daily revenue:** $2,500–$3,200 (at 4–7 jobs/day, depending on route)
- **Owner time on admin:** <10 hrs/week by end of year 1
