## 2. Independent Contractor Status

**2.1** Subcontractor is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or partner of Xcellent1 Lawn Care.

**2.2** Subcontractor controls their own schedule — when and which job blocks to accept through the platform. Company does not dictate hours, require exclusivity, or mandate minimum volume.

**2.3** Subcontractor may perform services for other companies and is free to operate their own separate lawn care business, provided they do not use Company client information to solicit business (see Section 8).

**2.4** Subcontractor is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes, including self-employment tax, income tax, and any applicable business licenses or permits. Company will issue IRS Form 1099-NEC for any calendar year in which total payments exceed $600.

**2.5** Subcontractor is not entitled to employee benefits: no workers' compensation (unless they carry their own), unemployment insurance, health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off, sick leave, or overtime pay.

## 3. Services & Scope

Subcontractor agrees to perform lawn care services as listed on individual job blocks claimed through the Xcellent1 platform. Standard services include:

- Mowing (at correct height for Louisiana warm-season grass per SOP)
- Trimming (fence lines, beds, structures)
- Edging (hard surfaces — sidewalks, driveways, curbs)
- Blowing (clippings and debris off hard surfaces)
- Haul-off (bagging and removing clippings/debris, when specified)
- Fertilization / weed control application (with applicable state licensing)
- Aeration and overseeding (seasonal)
- Seasonal cleanup

**Subcontractor agrees to:**
- Perform all work in compliance with Xcellent1's Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), as documented in SOP.md.
- Complete every claimed job block to the quality standard defined in the SOPs.
- Upload completion photos before marking a job complete in the platform app.

## 7. Performance Standards

**7.1** All work is evaluated against the quality standards in Xcellent1's SOPs. Key metrics:

| Metric | Standard | Consequence of Failure |
|---|---|---|
| On-Time Start | Within 15 min of scheduled window | Yellow/Red status |
| Quality Score | ≥95% (derived from photo review + client rating) | Yellow/Red status |
| Property Damage | Zero incidents | Immediate review |
| Block Abandonment | No unexcused misses | Removal from platform on repeat |

**7.2 Rating System:**
- **Green (≥95%):** Priority access to premium blocks, bonus payout opportunities.
- **Yellow (80–94%):** Warning sent, coaching offered. Two consecutive yellow periods may result in block queue restriction.
- **Red (<80%):** Suspended from claiming blocks. Owner reviews for reinstatement or permanent removal.

**7.3** Three unexcused block abandonments in a rolling 90-day period is grounds for permanent removal from the platform.

## 8. Confidentiality & Non-Solicitation

**8.1** Client information (names, addresses, phone numbers, property details, payment data) is Company confidential information. Subcontractor may use it only for the purpose of performing the specific job block.

**8.2** Subcontractor MUST NOT:
- Contact clients outside the Xcellent1 platform or app for any reason.
- Solicit, or attempt to solicit, any Xcellent1 client for personal business, including offering comparable lawn care services.
- Share client information with any third party.

**8.3** Violation of this section is grounds for immediate removal from the platform and liability for any damages or lost business resulting from the solicitation.
