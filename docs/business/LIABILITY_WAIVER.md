## 2. Property Damage Waiver

**2.1** The client acknowledges that Xcellent1 Lawn Care and its crew members/gig workers will exercise reasonable care. However, the following types of damage are specifically waived and will not be the basis for a claim:

- Damage to underground sprinkler heads, irrigation lines, low-voltage lighting wires, invisible fence wires, or utility cables that were not visibly marked or disclosed by the client before service.
- Damage to decorative lawn ornaments, landscaping features, or other objects that were not moved or secured by the client before service.
- Minor scuffing or marking on concrete, asphalt, brick, or stone hardscapes caused by edger contact — provided the crew used standard edging technique.
- Tire ruts or tracks on soft or wet turf, provided the crew attempted to avoid them under the conditions present.
- Damage caused by pre-existing conditions unknown to the provider at the time of service (rotted fence posts, cracked walkways, loose sprinkler heads).

**2.2** The provider WILL be responsible for damage caused by clear negligence: a crew member driving a mower into a fence, dropping equipment on a patio, or similar avoidable incidents. Such damage must be reported no later than 24 hours after service and documented with photos.

## 3. Personal Injury Waiver

**3.1** The client waives any claim for personal injury arising from lawn care services, including injury caused by:

- Flying debris (rocks, sticks, mulch, or other objects ejected from mowing/trimming equipment).
- Slips, trips, or falls on the property during or after service.
- Contact with equipment, tools, hoses, or vehicles on the premises.
- Exposure to fertilizer, herbicide, or pesticide applications applied according to label instructions and industry standards.

**3.2** THIS WAIVER DOES NOT APPLY to injury caused by the provider's gross negligence or intentional misconduct.

**3.3** The client is responsible for keeping all persons (including family members, visitors, tenants, and pets) at a safe distance from work areas during service. The provider will take reasonable precautions but cannot guarantee zero debris projection.

## 4. Chemical Use Acknowledgment

**4.1** If the client has opted in to fertilization or weed control services, the client acknowledges:

- Herbicides and pesticides are applied per manufacturer label instructions and applicable Louisiana Department of Agriculture & Forestry regulations.
- Some products may leave visible residue on grass, hard surfaces, or nearby objects for 24–48 hours after application.
- Pets and children should remain off treated areas until the product has dried completely (typically 2–4 hours, depending on weather conditions).
- The client should not water treated areas within 24 hours of application unless otherwise instructed by the applicator.
- The client should disclose any known allergies, chemical sensitivities, or beekeeping operations on the property before chemical application.

**4.2** The provider is NOT liable for:
- Weather-driven product performance (rain washing away treatment, heat stress on grass).
- Lawn discoloration, browning, or dieback that is consistent with normal product reaction, seasonal dormancy, or pre-existing lawn health conditions.
- Drift of product onto neighboring properties when applied according to label directions in normal weather conditions.
- Harm to plants, shrubs, or gardens that the client did not identify as off-limits before application.
