# Quick Assessment — Summary

A brief checklist for the 30–60 minute Quick Assessment workbook. The full,
detailed tab-by-tab instructions and examples are archived at
`docs/archive/assessment-instructions.full.md`.

Summary checklist:

- Gather last 3 months revenue & expenses
- Count recurring vs one-time customers
- Note equipment & crew inventory
- Identify lead sources and conversion rates
- Set 1-year and 3-year revenue goals

Complete the workbook for a tailored strategy and revenue projection; see the
archived file for detailed guidance.
