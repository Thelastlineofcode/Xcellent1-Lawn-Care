# Xcellent1 Owner Admin Pack

## Purpose
Use this pack to run the administrative side of Xcellent1 without guessing.
It pulls together owner-facing duties, hiring SOPs, and the market research
needed to get workers through the door.

## ERC Frame

### Empathize
- The business is small.
- Hiring is the bottleneck.
- The owner needs coverage fast, but the company cannot afford heavy HR overhead.
- Temp services are a fallback, not the default operating model.

### Realize
- Current live surfaces already exist:
  - owner dashboard
  - applications tracker
  - crew dashboard
  - client dashboard
  - invoices / payments
  - waitlist
- Current docs already define:
  - worker acquisition lanes
  - local wage floor
  - temp-service options
  - applicant SLAs

### Conceptualize
- Run the owner admin work from a few fixed loops:
  1. review applicants daily
  2. fill gaps with temp services when needed
  3. interview fast
  4. offer fast
  5. track hires and retention

## What we need from the owner

To actually manage hiring, we still need these decisions recorded in one place:
- target headcount for the next 30 / 60 / 90 days
- starting pay or pay range
- schedule preference: full-time, part-time, seasonal, or mixed
- work radius and service-area priorities
- vehicle / transportation requirement
- license / equipment requirement
- must-have experience vs trainable
- who can make hiring decisions
- preferred response tone for applicants
- temp-service agencies to call first
- interview availability window

## Administrative duties we can perform now

### Daily
- Review new applications
- Tag applicant source
- Reply within 24 hours
- Move strong candidates to interview
- Reject dead ends quickly

### Weekly
- Refresh hiring posts
- Review temp-service coverage gaps
- Schedule interviews
- Record offers / hires
- Review worker retention and no-shows

### When coverage breaks
- Call temp services before jobs slip
- Use temp labor for sick days, weather reschedules, and peak weeks
- Log which agency filled the shift and how the worker performed

## Hiring SOP

### 1. Attract
- Post the careers page as a real hiring funnel.
- Show pay range and schedule up front.
- Lead with simple language:
  - outdoor work
  - predictable pay
  - fast callback
  - room to grow

### 2. Capture
- Route every applicant into the applications table.
- Tag the source:
  - careers page
  - referral
  - temp service
  - community post
  - direct outreach

### 3. Screen
- Check availability.
- Check transportation.
- Check reliability.
- Reject fast if the basics are missing.

### 4. Interview
- Keep it short.
- Ask about:
  - work history
  - schedule
  - transportation
  - outdoor labor comfort
  - ability to follow SOPs
- Try to schedule within 48 hours.

### 5. Offer
- Make a clear starting role.
- Confirm first shift date.
- Confirm pay and payment method.

### 6. Onboard
- Verify identity.
- Walk the SOP.
- Test photo upload.
- Confirm crew contact path.

### 7. Retain
- Keep communication fast.
- Give predictable schedules.
- Ask for referrals.
- Promote reliable workers into lead roles.
