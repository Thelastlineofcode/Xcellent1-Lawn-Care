## Summary

Mobile-first interface for crew to view assigned jobs, update status, and upload
photos as proof of work.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Crew can view only assigned jobs.
- Jobs can be marked In-Progress and Completed.
- Photos uploaded attach to the job record and are viewable by Owner/Manager.
