Hey Lacardio — this is your complete start guide. Everything you need to know to hit the ground running: how to use the app, how to work a job from start to finish, and who to call when something goes wrong.

## What You’ll See on Your Dashboard

When you log in, you’ll see:

**Today’s Jobs** — a list of every job scheduled for today, in route order (closest first along the River Parishes corridor).

Each job card shows:
- Client name and address
- Services to perform (mowing, edging, etc.)
- Any notes (gate codes, dogs in yard, special requests)
- Job status: Assigned → In Progress → Complete

**Performance Badge** — top of your screen. Green means you’re on track. Yellow means something’s missing (a photo, a checklist step). Red means there’s an issue that needs attention.

---

## How to Work a Job (Step by Step)

### 1. Open the Job Card
Tap the job on your dashboard. You’ll see the full details — address, services, notes.

### 2. Clock In
Tap **"Clock In"** when you arrive at the property. This logs your start time. Do this before you unload anything.

### 3. Take a Before Photo
Before you touch the yard, take a photo of how it looks right now. Front of the property is fine. Tap **"Add Photo"** → select **"Before"**. Upload it.

### 4. Work the Checklist
For each service (mowing, edging, etc.), you’ll see a checklist of steps pulled from your SOPs. Check each step as you complete it. This protects you — if a client ever says something wasn’t done, your checklist is proof.

### 5. Take an After Photo
When the job is done, take a photo of the finished yard from the same spot as the before photo. Tap **"Add Photo"** → **"After"**. Upload it.

### 6. Clock Out
Tap **"Clock Out"** when you leave the property. This closes the job timer.

### 7. Mark Job Complete
Tap **"Mark Complete"**. This sends the client a notification that their job is done and shares the after photo with them automatically.

**That’s it — 7 steps, every job.**

## Services That Are Locked (License Required)

Do NOT perform these services — they require a Louisiana license that’s not yet on file:

| Service | Why Locked |
|---|---|
| Any pesticide / herbicide / weed killer spray | Requires LDAF Commercial Pesticide Applicator License |
| Irrigation install or repair | Requires LA State Plumbing Board license |
| Any fertilizer program (commercial) | Requires LDAF Restricted-Use license |
| Tree removal or climbing | Requires LA Horticulture Commission license |

If a client asks for one of these, tell them: **"That service requires a license I’m getting. I’ll let the owner know and we’ll follow up with you."** Then text Travone immediately.

These services will unlock in the app automatically once your licenses are on file.

## If Something Goes Wrong

| Situation | What To Do |
|---|---|
| Damage to client property | Stop work, take photos, text Travone immediately |
| Client is rude or threatening | Leave the property safely, text Travone |
| Equipment breaks down | Text Travone with the issue and your location |
| You’re running late | Text Travone your ETA so the client can be notified |
| Yard is in worse shape than expected | Take a photo before starting, text Travone |
| You find a hazard (wasp nest, flooding, gas smell) | Do not proceed, text Travone |
| App isn’t working | Text Travone with a screenshot |

**Travone’s number is in your phone.** When in doubt, text first, ask questions second.
