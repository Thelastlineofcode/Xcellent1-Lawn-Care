# Job Description: Communications & Notifications Coordinator

Hiring priority: P1 (can be combined with Intake initially)

## Role summary

This role maintains customer and owner communications: appointment reminders,
invoice emails, weekly owner digests, and ad-hoc alerts. They manage
SendGrid/Twilio templates, monitor delivery, and handle retries when automation
fails.

## Responsibilities

- Manage SendGrid transactional templates and Twilio SMS templates.
- Trigger reminders and owner alerts via the outbox system or manually when
  needed.
- Monitor delivery failures and work with Platform Engineer to resolve
  persistent issues.
- Maintain message content, test sends, and ensure language/localization where
  needed.

## Required skills & experience

- Strong copywriting for SMS and transactional email.
- Familiarity with SendGrid/Twilio dashboards and templates.
- Basic troubleshooting skills and ability to follow retry/playbook procedures.

## KPIs

- Delivery success rate
- Reminder no-show reduction
- Template quality score (owner feedback)

## Interview checklist

1. Show an example of a transactional email you templated (or write one live).
2. How would you handle a sudden spike in undelivered messages?
3. Describe how you'd localize a weekly digest for a Spanish-speaking owner
   (example steps).

## Onboarding (first 2 weeks)

1. Gain access to SendGrid and Twilio dashboards (staging keys first).
2. Review and test current templates and create a checklist for weekly digest
   content.
3. Run a simulated delivery test and document retry procedures.
