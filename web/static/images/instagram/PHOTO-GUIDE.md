# Instagram Photo Guide for Website

## Photos Needed from @xcellent1_lawn_care

Please download the following photos from your Instagram account and save them
to this directory (`web/static/images/instagram/`):

### 1. Hero Background Image (REQUIRED)

**Filename**: `hero-background.jpg`

- **What to look for**: Wide landscape shot of a beautiful, well-maintained lawn
- **Best options**: Freshly mowed lawn, full property view, or "before and
  after" showcase
- **Technical specs**: Should be high resolution (at least 1600x800px)
- **Usage**: Will be the main background image visitors see when they land on
  the site

### 2. Service Card Images (4 needed)

#### a) Weekly Lawn Maintenance

**Filename**: `service-mowing.jpg`

- **What to look for**: Mowing in action, freshly cut grass, or striped lawn
  pattern
- **Usage**: Card for "Weekly Lawn Maintenance" service

#### b) Landscaping & Design

**Filename**: `service-landscaping.jpg`

- **What to look for**: Beautiful landscaping work, flower beds, or garden
  design
- **Usage**: Card for "Landscaping & Design" service

#### c) Seasonal Services

**Filename**: `service-seasonal.jpg`

- **What to look for**: Fall cleanup, spring cleanup, or seasonal maintenance
  work
- **Usage**: Card for "Seasonal Services"

#### d) Fertilization & Treatment

**Filename**: `service-fertilization.jpg`

- **What to look for**: Healthy green lawn, fertilizer application, or weed
  control results
- **Usage**: Card for "Fertilization & Treatment" service

### 3. Blog/Portfolio Images (3 needed)

**Filenames**:

- `portfolio-1.jpg`
- `portfolio-2.jpg`
- `portfolio-3.jpg`

- **What to look for**: Your best work - dramatic transformations, beautiful
  results, happy customers
- **Usage**: Showcase recent projects in the blog/portfolio section

## How to Download Instagram Photos

1. Visit https://www.instagram.com/xcellent1_lawn_care/
2. For each photo you want to use:
   - Open the post
   - Right-click on the image
   - Select "Save image as..."
   - Save to this directory with the appropriate filename
3. Alternatively, use an Instagram download tool or save from your camera roll
   if you have the originals

## After Downloading

Once you've downloaded the photos to this directory, I'll automatically update
the website to use them. Just let me know when they're ready!
