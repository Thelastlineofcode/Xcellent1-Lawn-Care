Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?

No! We provide 2 weeks paid training. If you're reliable, physically fit, and willing to learn, we'll teach you the rest.

What's the interview process?

1) Submit application. 2) Phone screening (15 min). 3) In-person interview (30 min). 4) Offer within 24-48 hours.

Can I work part-time or weekends only?

Yes! We have seasonal and part-time positions. Many students work with us during summer.

What about winter?

We offer snow removal work, or you can take the season off and return in spring. Top performers stay year-round.

How fast can I get promoted?

Average time to crew lead is 6-12 months. We promote based on performance, not seniority.
