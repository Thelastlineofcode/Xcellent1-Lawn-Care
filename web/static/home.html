How much does lawn care cost? +

Our weekly lawn maintenance starts at $60/visit for standard residential yards. Pricing depends on yard size, terrain, and services needed. We offer free estimates ‚Äî just give us a call!

Do you offer one-time services? +

Yes! While we love our regular customers, we're happy to help with one-time cleanups, spring/fall services, or special projects. Just let us know what you need.

What's included in weekly lawn maintenance? +

Our standard service includes mowing, edging along sidewalks and driveways, string trimming around obstacles, and blowing off all hard surfaces. We leave your yard looking crisp and clean.

Do I need to be home when you come? +

Nope! Most of our customers aren't home during service. As long as we have access to the yard, we'll take care of everything. We'll send before & after photos so you can see the results.

How do I pay for services? +

We accept Cash App, Zelle, PayPal, and cash. We invoice after each service and payment is due within 7 days. Regular customers can also set up automatic payments.
